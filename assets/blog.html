Nature and Technology

Be it amoeba or be it whale,
sheer engineering smeared
in all who walk, fly or sail.

All the high-end gadgets and motors,
humbled by the efficiency of
teeny-tiny flagella's nano-scale rotor.

Nevertheless, we cracked
code to flight, and
eyes to remote sights, and,
ears to distant dears, and,
remedies to once world-fears.

So, let's join hand in hand,
for there's a lot to unfold,
until the uphill battle against time
our mortal bodies uphold.

Motivation

After a stormy chilly night,
inducing shivers down the spine,
only then we realize
how warm is the morning sunshine.

so, inhaling a deep breath,
get working with a faith in orchestra
not that evident,
yet, so divine.

Behold, what we perceive
as mere normal
is in fact,
eight coincides so so pristine.

Compliment

Originally from divine galaxy,
landed on this mortal spehere

Given the wonderness you hold,
how can one help to not-stare.
